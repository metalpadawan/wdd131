Classic Anime Archive

Site Name

Classic Anime Archive

I chose this name because the website will focus on older anime series and films. The word "archive" suggests a curated collection of information, recommendations, and historical context for visitors who want to explore anime from earlier decades.

Optional domain: classic-anime-archive.org

Site Purpose

The site will introduce visitors to classic anime by providing short summaries, release information, genre and decade categories, creator or studio highlights, and beginner-friendly recommendations. The site will also include an interactive recommendation area where users can filter titles and save favorites.

Scenarios

  • Which classic anime should I watch first if I am new to older anime?
  • Can I filter classic anime by decade, genre, or audience type?
  • Which studios and creators helped shape classic anime storytelling and style?

Color Scheme

Deep Teal - #123c4a

Used for the page header, section headings, and primary navigation areas.

Archive Coral - #c94f4f

Used for links, active states, accents, and important calls to action.

Film Gold - #f2c14e

Used for logo details, borders, highlights, and small decorative accents.

Soft Paper - #f6f8fb

Used for the main page background to keep content readable and calm.

Typography

  • Merriweather: used for the site name, page headings, and major section headings.
  • Roboto: used for body text, navigation, cards, buttons, and form controls.

Planned Pages

  • index.html: home page with featured classics and a short introduction.
  • titles.html: searchable or filterable anime title guide by decade and genre.
  • recommendations.html: recommendation form, saved favorites, and watch suggestions.
  • references.html: image and text source references.
